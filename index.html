ABOUT TENJIKU BYTES

Founded in 2018, Tenjiku Bytes Studio is an independent game development powerhouse with teams across Tokyo, Singapore, and Los Angeles. Our name "Tenjiku" comes from ancient Japanese references to distant, exotic lands - representing our boundless creativity and global perspective.

We specialize in crafting innovative gaming experiences that blend Eastern and Western design philosophies. Our team of 50+ developers, artists, and storytellers are united by a passion for pushing technological and creative boundaries.
